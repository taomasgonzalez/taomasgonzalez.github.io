About Me

I am a Senior Software Engineer at arm, UK's biggest semiconductor company on a mission to build the future of computing. I enjoy training neural networks, writing efficient code and thinking holistically, focusing on optimization where it adds real value.

In my personal time, I develop projects and study topics at the intersection of Artificial Intelligence, Biology, and Mathematics. I'm very mathematically inclined and often delve into Linear Algebra, Spectral analysis, Time series, Stochastic Signals, etc. My portfolio of AI projects is growing rapidly and includes work on multi-omics (transcriptomics + histology) using GNNs, ECG processing and even self-playing videogames.

I did my MSc in Electronic Engineering at ITBA, specializing in Signal Processing, where I've also studied bioengineering and chemistry.

I contribute to several open source projects under taomasgonzalez and tgonzalezorlandoarm.

I was born and raised in Buenos Aires, Argentina 🇦🇷, I'm italian 🇮🇹 and I'm living in the UK 🇬🇧
